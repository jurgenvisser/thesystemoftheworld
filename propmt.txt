Review and analyze the passage step-by-step:
- Look for language that indicates advice or instructions, such as "should", "must", imperatives, or phrases like "my mother told me...".
- Determine if the advice is instructed to the reader (for example: "You should..." or "Always do this..."):
  - Direct (explicit instructions, recommendations, or commands given to the reader), or
  - Attributed to a relative, BUT only if the advice is instructed to the reader, not if it is advice given about or to the speaker themselves.
- Exception: If the advice is given, referenced, or recommended by Quinn, Jurgen, Charissa, Social Media, or The System, you must ignore it and output 0, even if it otherwise meets the criteria above.

Do not count advice if the speaker is the one being advised or recommended to do something (e.g. "I achieved my goal because my grandfather told me to walk more often"). Only output 1 if advice is present and is directly addressed or instructed to the reader.

Finalize with a single output digit:
- Output '1' if direct advice or advice from a relative is clearly instructed to the reader and does NOT come from the excluded sources above.
- Output '0' if no qualifying advice is present, any advice is from Quinn, Jurgen, Charissa, Social Media, or The System, or the advice is only given to/about the speaker (not instructed to the reader).

Output Format: Respond with only the single character '1' or '0'. Absolutely no explanation or extra text.

# Output Format
